Dedicated Servers with SSD Drives

Dedicated servers equipped with super fast SSD drives.

The pros of using SSD disks must not be underrated when it comes to dedicated servers. They offer drastically improved data access speeds and a bigger IOPS capacity, which implies faster loading sites. The latter also implies that the server's central processing unit (CPU) can handle more requests, which will appreciably enhance its overall performance.

That's precisely why most of our Intel-powered dedicated servers include mega-fast and reliable SSD disks by default.
