Domain WHOIS Privacy Protection

Take care of your domain info from the common visitor. Make use of the Whois Privacy Protection service.

Whois Privacy Protection with Flex Hosting LLC

In case you’d like to get an additional layer of protection for your domain name and in addition shield it against online theft and various other Internet attacks, our Domain WHOIS Privacy Protection service can be a very good solution. It will efficiently protect all of your personal information, that is required to stay freely available in the domain’s WHOIS information, by simply changing it using our details.
Your current private info will not be the sole thing defended. We are going to also sort all unwelcome e–mails and deliver merely domain–related email messages to your mailboxes.

Whois Privacy Protection–eligible TLDs

A result of a registry–imposed constraint, Flex Hosting LLC are unable to have the Whois Privacy Protection solution to all domain names available. When a TLD supports this type of solution by registry govern, only then we can supply it to you also. Nevertheless, much of the well–known domains extensions feature Whois Privacy Protection support.
These TLDs include: .com, .net, .org, .info, .biz, .co, .co.za, .me, .tv, .cc and .we.bs.

SSL Certificates

Get hold of SSL certificates straight from your CP

Through the Domain Manager, you’ll also be able to activate an SSL certificate for your domain. With an SSL certificate, all the sensitive information that your customers submit via your web site (through the order form, via a login form, and so on) will be enciphered and the sent information will never be put at risk.

To enable an SSL certificate for your domain, simply fill in the necessary information and press the Activate button. You can request an ordinary SSL certificate, which will be valid for one single host, or a wildcard SSL certificate, which will be valid for all the hosts under your domain.
