What is WordPress?

Surpassing 46.000.000 yearly downloads, WordPress at this time is the system behind over 18% of all web sites that are currently online. It is the most famous blogging platform available and over time it’s become something much more. Anyone can develop any sort of website with WordPress, from an e–commerce online shop to a portfolio website, with simply a couple of mouse clicks. And the best part is that everything’s free.

Furthermore, WordPress also offers very good community support. You’ll find lots of no cost WordPress web templates and plugins available out there and also a large number of sites which are dedicated only to WordPress.
